Let's get DeepThis poem is dedicated to my bestfriend and dad who loves me even when I am annoying
Him

The sound of laughter and community echoed the streets of his childhood home Born from a big family in an opressing time he knew nothing but love and freedom! His feet scrambling around daily looking for the next adventure. With rules stopping him because of the pigment of his skin, He braved the world and lived despite it. He was free of all walls that made him. He dreams of life ahead and billions of posibilities He dreams of the inner life of his castle, the one formed at his touch. Music bounces from within. He sees a million sunrises and the starry nights that follow, each as crisp and perfect as the last. And then the moment blossoms into a fresh one.

Her

In his dreams he heard the sound of his child's feet, her laughter and impromptu song lyrics. He would be so asleep and so awake in his soul, reliving those perfect moments of fatherhood. There were days he'd recall those adventures of the nighttime and lavish them upon me over breakfast, no doubt with a few beautiful details added on the fly. And he looked so wise as he slept, that steady heart, those steady breaths, more than enough to make me love him all over again. His humor often caused my smile to burts into laughter, if only he knew what joy it brought me to see him happy. I could never have asked for anyone better. He, He is my dad.
